Projects that showcase my QA expertise

Throughout my experience at Asuransi Astra, I’ve been trusted to ensure the quality and functionality of various high-impact digital products in the insurance sector — from vehicle and health insurance platforms to specialized tech-integrated services like screen crack insurance with TikTok Nano. I played a key role in end-to-end testing, UAT, and real-world scenario validations across mobile and web applications. My contribution helped ensure timely and stable product delivery, while also identifying critical edge cases that weren’t covered in initial test scripts. These projects not only sharpened my QA skills but also strengthened my ability to work closely with cross-functional teams, meet tight deadlines, and deliver reliable digital solutions aligned with business goals.

Gardaoto

GardaOto is a platform designed for 4-wheeler vehicle insurance users. My role in this project was to ensure that the platform met high-quality standards through thorough testing. Key Responsibilities and Contributions:

  • Test case creation: Developed comprehensive test cases covering different aspects of the project to ensure all features functioned as expected.
  • Code migration to testing environment: Managed the migration of code to the testing environment, ensuring that the WordPress-based project was ready for thorough testing.
  • End-to-End testing for TSI Slider Enhancement: Conducted functionality, usability, compatibility, regression, and database testing to validate data accuracy.
  • Bug analysis : Investigated and analyzed bugs, ensuring proper documentation and effective communication with the development team for swift resolutions.
  • User Acceptance Testing (UAT) : Conducted UAT to ensure that the product met all requirements from the user's perspective and provided a smooth experience.

Impact: Through meticulous testing and analysis, I helped deliver the GardaOto project on time, ensuring it met quality and functionality expectations. By uncovering special test cases during testing, I played a key role in refining the product before release.

Screencrack Insurance is an innovative service offered through a partnership with Nano TikTok, providing insurance for screen damage. The system processes data in bulk using a scheduler, and I was responsible for ensuring its functionality and integration with TikTok’s system. Key Responsibilities and Contributions:

  • Key Responsibilities and Contributions: Conducted functionality testing, database testing, and performance testing on the scheduler system to ensure it could process large amounts of data efficiently.
  • Bug analysis: Analyzed and documented bugs to ensure that any issues were communicated clearly to the development team and addressed promptly.
  • System Integration Testing with Nano TikTok: Coordinated integration testing with Nano TikTok to ensure seamless communication and data processing between systems.
  • User Acceptance Testing (UAT) with Client: Worked closely with the client to ensure that the final product met their needs and expectations, ensuring a smooth user experience.
  • Documentation Creation: Produced detailed documentation for the project, helping the team maintain clarity throughout the development process.

Impact: My testing efforts ensured that the Screencrack Insurance service worked smoothly across the entire system, meeting client requirements and delivering a functional product on time. My contributions helped guarantee that the system could handle bulk data processing, contributing to the overall efficiency of the service.
