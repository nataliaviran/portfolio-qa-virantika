How I Got Here

My journey in Software Quality Assurance began with a deep interest in technology and a desire to understand how things work beneath the surface. With over 3 years of hands-on experience in manual testing, I have had the opportunity to work on diverse projects, from vehicle insurance applications to health tech platforms. Each project provided new challenges, but through consistent learning, detailed analysis, and a meticulous approach, I have grown into a reliable QA professional who can confidently test across multiple platforms such as web, Android, and iOS. I have honed my skills in testing methodologies, test case creation, and conducting thorough regression, usability, and database testing.

My Values & Beliefs

Quality Over Quantity

I believe that the quality of a product is far more important than the number of features or scope. Focusing on delivering a few well-executed features rather than a large number of poorly tested ones ensures that the product remains reliable and provides the best user experience.

Attention to Detail

Small issues, if overlooked, can lead to bigger problems later on. I focus on the fine details and consistently look for edge cases during testing. This thoroughness ensures that potential issues, which may not be immediately obvious, are caught early in the development process.

Transparency and Open Communication

I value open, honest communication with my team and stakeholders. I believe that discussing issues and potential improvements early in the development cycle prevents surprises down the road and fosters collaboration. Transparency helps ensure everyone is on the same page and aligned with the project goals.

Collaboration

I see QA as a team effort. Working together with developers, business analysts, and product managers allows for a more comprehensive understanding of the product requirements, which leads to better testing and more accurate results. Collaboration ensures that all perspectives are considered, leading to a product that meets both technical and business expectations.

Continuous Learning

The tech industry is always evolving, and I believe in staying current with new testing tools, methodologies, and industry trends. Whether it’s learning about automation tools, testing techniques, or updates in software development, I am committed to ongoing learning to enhance my skills and deliver the best quality assurance results.

User-Centric Testing

My testing is always focused on the end-user experience. I understand that a product must not only function correctly but also be easy and intuitive for users. I prioritize usability testing and ensure that products are user-friendly and meet the expectations of the target audience.
